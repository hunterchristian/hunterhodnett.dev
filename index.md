I've spent 14 years building software in the consumer technology industry — 9 as an engineer at places like Reddit, Amazon Music, and The Home Depot, and the last 5 as a founder.

These days I lead product and engineering at Chipp, where we're making it easy for anyone to create and deploy AI-powered apps.

## What does Hunter work on?

Hunter is the CPTO and Co-founder of **Chipp**, a no-code AI platform serving over 16,000 builders with more than 80,000 apps reaching 36 million users. Chipp brings software margins to service industries by connecting non-traditional software buyers with pre-built AI tooling that fits their niche use cases.

Before Chipp, Hunter founded **Cottage**, a marketplace that crowd-sourced MVPs for non-technical startup founders while providing junior engineers with real-world coding experience. Cottage laid the groundwork for Chipp by exploring how to bridge the gap between non-traditional software buyers and SaaS solutions.

## Career

- **Chipp — CPTO and Co-founder** (August 2023 – Present, CTO → CPTO): No-code AI platform serving over 16,000 builders with more than 80,000 apps reaching 36 million users. Like ChatGPT and Wix had a baby. Bringing software margins to service industries by connecting non-traditional software buyers with pre-built AI tooling that fits their niche use cases. Using AI to stack development leverage while building an AI platform.
- **Ohai.ai — Lead Software Engineer** (November 2022 – June 2023): Brought ed-tech knowledge to Ohai, a learning platform. Left during the pivot to build Chipp.ai with colleague Scott Meyer after realizing the gen-AI wave could bring software margins to service businesses.
- **Cottage — Founder, CEO** (June 2021 – November 2022): Crowd-sourced MVPs for non-technical startup founders while providing junior engineers with real-world coding experience. Clients posted Figma designs, Cottage broke them into component-level competitions, and junior engineers competed to build the best component using React.js, Next.js, and TypeScript. Winning solutions deployed into Softr.io no-code apps as micro-frontends. First attempt at bridging the gap between non-traditional software buyers and SaaS — ultimately laid the groundwork for Chipp.
- **The Home Depot — Coding Bootcamp Instructor** (April 2019 – February 2021): Taught a 16-week coding bootcamp that converted Home Depot associates into full-stack software engineers. Built an e-learning platform that retrained 290 software engineers in 3 months on isomorphic rendering with React.js and Node.js.
- **Reddit — Software Engineer** (July 2017 – December 2018): Helped build the new reddit.com with React.js, Redux, and TypeScript. Created a tool to programmatically measure UI rendering performance across ~15 engineers and incorporated it into CI. The tool was built into an executive dashboard that informed the C-suite on launch readiness of the redesigned site.
- **Amazon Music — Software Development Engineer** (July 2015 – July 2017): Helped launch Amazon Music Unlimited. Created a tool to programmatically identify performance regressions in release candidates and incorporated it into the release pipeline. Also helped build the redesigned Amazon Music Desktop player as a hybrid web application.
